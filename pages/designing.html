OpenRocket is used by rocketeers to design and test a model rocket design before actually building and flying it. The software accurately computes the aerodynamic properties of rockets and simulates their flight, returning a wide range of technical results.

This page will teach you to design a basic rocket using OpenRocket. Read through the entire page or jump to various sections of the rocket by clicking picture of the coinciding rocket design below.

 

To be successful, each rocket must have the following elements.

  • Nose Cone
  • Body Tube
  • Fins
  • Motor Mount, which may contain centering rings, an inner tube, hook/retainer, and/or motor block
  • launch lug
  • shock cord
  • parachute or streamer
